About Me

Getting to know Justin Williams

Welcome to my e-portfolio! My name is Justin Williams, and I'm an 18-year-old Finance major from Miami, Florida, currently studying at Florida State University. This semester, I've had the incredible opportunity to study abroad in Valencia, Spain, where I've been enrolled in the ENC2135 course that inspired this digital portfolio.

Growing up in Miami, I was immersed in the world of real estate from an early age. My mother owns Miami Connections Realty, a real estate brokerage that exposed me to everything from property showings and negotiations to deal closings. This firsthand experience sparked my passion for both finance and real estate, and I've been fascinated by how these industries intersect and evolve with technology.

I'm particularly interested in how digital transformation is reshaping the real estate and finance sectors. Social media, data analytics, and emerging technologies are revolutionizing how professionals connect, communicate, and collaborate in these fields. Through this portfolio, you'll find research projects, articles, and creative work that explore these themes while demonstrating my growth as both a student and aspiring professional.

This e-portfolio showcases the academic and creative work I've completed during my time in Valencia, reflecting not only my coursework but also the unique perspective I've gained from studying abroad and experiencing European culture firsthand.
